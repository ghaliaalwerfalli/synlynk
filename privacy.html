How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Orba is stored locally on your iPhone using AsyncStorage, which utilises Apple's NSUserDefaults system under the hood. This data is sandboxed to the Orba app and is not accessible to other apps on your device.

The following local storage keys are used by the App:

  • @orba_entries — your financial log entries
  • @orba_profile — your name, spending limit, and income target
  • @orba_custom_cats — your category list (built-in + custom)
  • @orba_theme — your dark / light mode preference
  • @orba_schema — internal schema version for migration

None of this data is transmitted anywhere. The App contains no networking code related to user data.

Apple Privacy Manifest

Orba includes an Apple Privacy Manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) as required for App Store submission. It declares the following:

  • NSPrivacyTracking: false — the App does not track users
  • NSPrivacyCollectedDataTypes: [] (empty — no data collected)
  • NSPrivacyAccessedAPICategoryUserDefaults — accessed solely to store your app preferences and entries locally on your device, per Apple's permitted use category "CA92.1"
  • NSPrivacyAccessedAPICategoryFileTimestamp — used by the React Native framework for file operations; no timestamp data is collected or transmitted

No third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising frameworks, or crash reporters are included in Orba.

Network Activity

Orba makes zero network requests related to your data. The App functions entirely offline. You may use Orba with aeroplane mode enabled and all features will work identically.

The only network activity Orba may perform is loading Google Fonts at app startup — a standard iOS behaviour for React Native apps using web fonts. This request does not include any personal or financial data. If you are offline, the App uses system fallback fonts.

Third Parties

Orba does not share your information with any third party because there is no information to share. There are no advertising partners, no data brokers, no analytics providers, and no cloud infrastructure associated with this App.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple's own privacy practices apply to the App Store experience itself. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about Apple's data practices.

Children's Privacy

Orba does not collect any personal information from anyone, including users under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, the App does not present risks under COPPA or similar children's privacy regulations.

Deleting Your Data

You have complete control over your data at all times:

  • Delete all entries: In the App, go to Me → Settings → Delete all entries. This erases your entire log while preserving your profile and budget settings.
  • Delete the App: Uninstalling Orba from your iPhone removes all associated local data permanently. iOS does not retain AsyncStorage data after app deletion.
  • iPhone settings: You may also clear app storage from iPhone Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Orba → Delete App.

Because we hold no data on our side, there is no "request deletion from our servers" step — your data is only ever on your device.
